Information You Choose to Display Publicly on the Tiwun

Some users may elect to publicly post personally identifying or sensitive information about themselves in their normal use of the Tiwun. This could occur through use of the optional profile fields, classifieds or comments. Information like that, which is voluntarily posted in publicly visible parts of the Tiwun, is considered to be public, even if it would otherwise be considered to be personally identifying or sensitive. As such, it is not subject to the protocols listed below, because we don’t control it; you do. Additionally, voluntarily publicizing such information means that you lose any privacy rights you might normally have with regards to that information. It also increases your chances of receiving unwanted communications, like spam.
